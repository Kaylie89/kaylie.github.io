Unit 4: Application of Threat Modelling and Management Techniques

In this unit, we explored practical applications of threat modelling and management techniques. We were guided by essential resources such as the Threat Modelling Manifesto, the OWASP Threat Modelling Cookbook, and the Mitre ATT&CK database.

Key Concepts Covered

  • Threat Modelling Tools and Resources: Discussion on various tools and resources for threat modelling and management.
  • Publicly Available Tools: Evaluation of the use of publicly available tools to assist with threat modelling.
  • Threat Model Creation: Creation of threat models based on suggested scenarios.

This unit emphasized the importance of selecting appropriate tools for different threat scenarios and critically evaluating their effectiveness. Below is the reflective piece on this unit:

Reflection on Unit 4

This unit provided practical experience in threat modelling and management, utilizing key resources such as the Threat Modelling Manifesto, OWASP Cookbook, and the Mitre ATT&CK database. The hands-on activities helped me understand the importance of selecting appropriate tools for different threat scenarios and critically evaluating their effectiveness. This experience is invaluable for my future role in cybersecurity, where threat modelling is essential for proactive risk management.
